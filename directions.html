Directions

Pineland Marina

13921 Waterfront Dr, Bokeelia, FL 33922

Located on the western shore of Pine Island, Pineland Marina is the largest full service marina on Pine Island and your easy access destination to the Point House. To learn more, please call 239.283.3593 or visit them online. When using Island Girl Charters, the parking at the marina is $8 per day.

From the north:
  • I-75 S toward Tampa
  • Take exit 161 for Charlotte County 768 toward Punta Gorda/Charlotte Co Airport
  • Use the right 2 lanes to turn right onto Jones Loop Rd (signs for Punta Gorda/Edison College/County Rd 768)
  • Continue straight to stay on Jones Loop Rd
  • Continue onto Burnt Store Rd
  • Turn right onto SW Pine Island Rd
  • Turn right onto Pine Island Blvd/Stringfellow Rd
  • Turn left onto Pineland Rd
  • Continue onto Waterfront Dr
  • Destination will be on the left
From the south:
  • I-75 N (Partial Toll Road)
  • Take exit 136 toward Ft Myers/Lehigh Acres/FL-884
  • Use the left 2 lanes to turn left onto FL-884 W/Colonial Blvd (signs for Ft Myers/Cape Coral)
  • Keep left to continue on Colonial Blvd
  • Continue straight to stay on Colonial Blvd (Toll Road)
  • Continue onto Midpoint Bridge (Toll Road)
  • Continue onto Veterans Memorial Pkwy/Veterans Pkwy (Toll Road)
  • Continue straight to stay on Veterans Memorial Pkwy/Veterans Pkwy (Toll Road)
  • Keep left to stay on Veterans Memorial Pkwy/Veterans Pkwy (Toll Road)
  • Turn left onto SW Pine Island Rd
  • Turn right onto Pine Island Blvd/Stringfellow Rd
  • Turn left onto Pineland Rd
  • Continue onto Waterfront Dr
  • Destination will be on the right

Pineland Marina to Point House

4201 Point House Trail, Captiva, FL 33924

Island Girl Charters

Island Girl Charters at Pineland Marina is the water taxi service to North Captiva Island, docking at Safety Harbor Club and Barnacle Island Restaurant, reservations are recommended. Call 239.633.8142 or book online today.

Island Golf Cart Rentals

When taking the Island Girl Charters across have them drop you off at Barnacles Restaurant, the golf cart company will have a cart waiting there for you. Call ahead to make a reservation at 239.440.7162.

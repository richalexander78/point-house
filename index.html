Straight to the Point.

North Captiva is a bridgeless barrier island in Southwest Florida, located west of Pine Island in the Gulf of Mexico. The Point House is a classic Florida beach house built on a private peninsula at the entrance to Safety Harbor. With 360 degrees of spectacular views, it's the perfect setting for rest, relaxation, birding and beachcombing. A private, protected deep water dock for your boat, along with outstanding fishing, are the makings of an angler’s dream. Your stay at the Point House is sure to be a memorable one—one that keeps you coming back.
